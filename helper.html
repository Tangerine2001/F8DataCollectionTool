helper.grab_current_data(client: client.Client, card: str) → tuple[dict, dict, dict]

Grabs the current data from the passed in card.

Parameters

  • client – Client object from client

  • card – The card to read history from

Returns

Tuple of 3 dicts, each dict represents live, minute, and daily data respectively.

Access the information in them with [‘label’] for all the labeling information. Other information: [‘receivingLow’, ‘receivingMean’, ‘receivingHigh’] [‘transmittingLow’, ‘transmittingMean’, ‘transmittingHigh’]
